Frequently Asked Questions

what are solar panels?

Simply put Solar panels are devices that convert light into electricity. solar panel works by allowing photons, or particles of light, to knock electrons free from atoms, generating a flow of electricity.

What is peak sun hours?

Peak sun-hours refers specifically to how much sun exposure is usable for efficient energy production in an area during a typical day. A peak sun-hour, specifically, is an hour during which the intensity of sunlight is 1,000 watts per square meter.

What are household appliances?

Household appliances are home equipments that make use of electricity to function. Examples are Tv set, refrigerator, LED bulbs etc

What is wattage in electricity?

Wattage is the amount of energy it takes for an electric equipment to function. This varies depending on the appliance, a refrigerator consumes far more wattage than an LED bulb.
